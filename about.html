About Us

Atelier Palm is the event venue landing page for Sweet Palm Coffee & Food—bringing coffee culture and gathering-friendly space together on King Street.

A space for coffee + connection

By day, we’re a welcoming coffee shop experience in the heart of Charleston. Off hours, we shift into a flexible venue for private gatherings, brand moments, workshops, and coffee-forward events. The goal is simple: create an atmosphere that feels elevated but easy—where guests can connect, celebrate, and enjoy a curated experience that still feels like your favorite local spot.
