About Us

Atelier Palm is a catering service branch of Sweet Palm Coffee & We bring coffee culture and to your local event space.

A space for coffee + catering service to go

By day, we’re a welcoming coffee shop experience in the heart of Charleston. Off hours, we shift into a flexible venue and catering service for private gatherings, brand moments, workshops, and coffee-forward events. The goal is simple: create an atmosphere that feels elevated but easy—where guests can connect, celebrate, and enjoy a curated experience that still feels like your favorite local spot.
